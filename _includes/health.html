Tree health

(More text needed) Having thousands and thousands of trees standing around in a harsh environment as a major city like NYC, makes one wonder what will happens with them and their surroundings, since they are dynamic things that change over time. Trees can get sick and die, fall over and into power and phone lines, roads etc. as well as be a nuisance for the people living near them if they start looking old or sick.
Because of this, there can be value gained from knowing the health status of the trees around the city. Below is a plot of the health status of the top 20 trees. It is worth nothing, however, that the dataset is based on a community survey, so the basis for the trees' health is mostly based on looks and how the person surveying the tree perceives the tree as a whole.
